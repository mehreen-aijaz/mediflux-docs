The **Referrals** tab lets you invite other pharmacy owners to MediFlux using your unique referral code or link, and earn rewards when they register.
---

## Referral Program Details

You'll find your **Referral Code** and a corresponding **Referral Link**, which can be shared directly with other store owners.

| Option | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Copy Code** | Copies your referral code |
| **Copy** | Copies your referral link |
| **Share** | Opens sharing options for your referral link |

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share Your Code">
    Send your referral code or link to fellow pharmacy and healthcare store owners.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They Register">
    When registering their store, they enter your referral code in the sign-up form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn Rewards">
    Get premium features, subscription discounts, or account credits upon verification.
  </Step>
</Steps>
