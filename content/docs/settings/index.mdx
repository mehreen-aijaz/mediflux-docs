The **Settings** section is where you configure how MediFlux works for your pharmacy, from your store's business details and bank information to invoice numbering, notifications, integrations, and who on your team has access. It's not part of the main sidebar; instead, you'll find it under your **profile icon** in the top-right corner of any screen.

---

## Accessing Settings

Click the **profile icon** in the top-right corner to open a dropdown with:

| Option | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Profile** | Your personal user profile |
| **Settings** | Opens the Settings section |
| **Changelog** | Recent product updates |
| **Logout** | Signs you out of MediFlux |

Click **Settings** to get started.

## Navigating the Settings section

Once inside Settings, eleven tabs run across the top of the page:

| Tab | What it's for |
| --- | --- |
| **Account** | Your store's business details, contact information, bank details, and store assets like logo, signature, and stamp |
| **Modules** | Enable or disable MediFlux modules for your store |
| **Invoice Counter** | Configure invoice numbering for your bills |
| **Notifications** | Manage notification preferences |
| **Bill Templates** | Customize how your bills and invoices look |
| **Integrations** | Connect MediFlux with other tools and services |
| **User Management** | Manage staff accounts and their access |
| **Data Management** | Manage your store's data |
| **Subscriptions** | View and manage your MediFlux subscription plan |
| **Accounting Settings** | Configure accounting-related preferences |
| **Referrals** | Manage referral links and rewards |
