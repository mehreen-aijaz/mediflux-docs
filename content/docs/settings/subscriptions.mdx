The **Subscriptions** tab shows your store's current plan status and lets you upgrade or renew to a longer-term plan for better savings.

---

## Current subscription status

At the top, you'll see whether you have an active subscription, along with:

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Status** | Active or otherwise |
| **Activated** | Date your current plan was activated |
| **Payment** | Payment method used (e.g., Online) |
| **Expires On** | Date your current plan expires |

## Subscription Plans

Below your current status, MediFlux offers multiple subscription plans to upgrade or renew into. Each plan shows its price, effective monthly cost, and included features, such as multi-user access, unlimited transactions, priority support, and live customer support.

<Note>
Plan durations, pricing, and features shown here are subject to change. Always refer to the in-app Subscriptions page for the most current plans and pricing.
</Note>

Click **Upgrade Now** on any plan to proceed.

  src="/images/subscriptions-plans.png"

  description="Available plans with pricing, features, and Upgrade Now options."

## FAQs

**What happens if my subscription expires?**
Refer to the Subscriptions page for details on what features may be affected, since this can change depending on your plan and current MediFlux policies.

**Do longer-term plans offer better savings?**
Yes, generally, longer commitments (e.g., 3-year or 5-year plans) come at a lower effective monthly cost compared to shorter plans.
