The **Account** tab holds your store's core business information — used across invoices, bills, and other documents generated by MediFlux. This is also where you manage your store's logo, signature, and stamp.

---

## Account Info

At the top, you'll see your store's name, plan status (e.g., **Pro Active**, with expiry date), and:

| Section | Fields |
| --- | --- |
| **Contact** | Phone, Email |
| **Location** | Address |
| **Bank Details** | Bank, Account Name, Account Number, IFSC |

Click **Edit** to open the **Edit Store Details** form.

## Editing store details

The **Edit Store Details** form is split into three sections:

<Figure
  src="/images/edit-store-details-form.png"
  title="Edit Store Details"
  description="Form split into Store Information, Address, and Bank Details sections."
/>

### Store Information

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Store Name** | Your pharmacy's registered name |
| **Owner Name** | Name of the store owner |
| **Phone** | Contact number, with a separate **Edit** option |
| **Email** | Contact email, with a separate **Edit** option |

### Address

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Address Line 1** | Street address |
| **Locality** | Locality or area |
| **State** | State |
| **Pincode** | Postal code |

### Bank Details

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Bank Name** | Name of the bank |
| **Branch Name** | Branch name |
| **IFSC** | Bank's IFSC code |
| **Account Number** | Bank account number |
| **Account Name** | Name on the account |
| **UPI ID** | UPI ID used for payments |

Click **Save** to update your details, or **Cancel** to discard changes.

<Figure
  src="/images/edit-store-details-form-filled.png"
  title="Edit Store Details Form"
  description="All fields filled in — Store Information, Address, and Bank Details."
/>

<Note>
Phone and Email have their own **Edit** option within the form, separate from the rest of the store details, since changing these may require additional verification.
</Note>

---

## Store Assets

Below Account Info, three collapsible sections let you manage documents used on your invoices and bills:

| Asset | What it's for |
| --- | --- |
| **Logo** | Your store's logo, shown on invoices and bills |
| **Signature** | A signature image, used on documents that require sign-off |
| **Stamp** | Your store's official stamp, used on documents that require it |

Click on any asset to expand it and view the current file. Click the asset again to open the **Change Asset** dialog, where you can drag and drop a file or browse to upload a new one (up to 2MB).

<Figure
  src="/images/store-assets.png"
  title="Store Assets"
  description="Logo, Signature, and Stamp sections with upload and change options."
/>

## FAQs

**Is there a file size limit for Logo, Signature, or Stamp uploads?**
Yes, uploads are limited to 2MB per file.

**Can I change my store's phone number or email directly from the Edit form?**
Yes, both fields have a separate **Edit** option next to them within the Edit Store Details form.
