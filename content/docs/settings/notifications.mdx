The **Notifications** tab controls how you receive alerts from MediFlux, such as updates on stock, orders, and other store activity.

---

## Push Notifications

Toggle **Push Notifications** on or off to control whether you receive browser or device notifications from MediFlux.

The page shows your **Current Status**:

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Active** | Notifications are enabled and working |
| **Inactive** | Notifications are not enabled |

---

## FAQs

**Why would I turn Push Notifications off?**
If you don't want browser or device alerts from MediFlux and prefer to check updates manually within the app, you can turn this off.
