The **Re-orders** feature in MediFlux helps you handle repeat purchases from your regular customers quickly and efficiently. Instead of creating a new sale from scratch every time, you can re-order based on previous transactions.

---

## Coming Soon

This section is currently under development and will be updated with full documentation shortly.

Planned features include:

- **Quick re-order from history** — Re-create a sale based on a customer's previous purchase with one click
- **Recurring order setup** — Set up automatic re-orders for patients on long-term medication
- **Re-order reminders** — Get notified when a regular customer is due for their next purchase
- **Bulk re-ordering** — Process multiple re-orders at once for wholesale or institutional customers
