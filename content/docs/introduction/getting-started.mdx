This guide walks you through setting up **MediFlux** for the first time, from creating your store to making your first sale. Whether you prefer a quick self-service setup or guided onboarding with our support team, you'll be up and running within minutes.

---

## What you need

**MediFlux** is a web-based PWA that runs on any device with a browser and internet connection. **No downloads or local servers are required.**

| Component | Minimum | Recommended |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Operating System** | Any modern OS | Windows 10+, macOS, Linux, Android, iOS |
| **Processor** | Any dual-core CPU | Intel Core i3 or above |
| **RAM** | 2 GB | 4 GB or above |
| **Internet** | Basic connectivity (2 Mbps) | Stable 4G or broadband (5–10 Mbps) |
| **Browser** | Latest Chrome, Firefox, Edge | Chrome (latest version) |

Even pharmacies in remote areas with modest connectivity and basic desktops can operate efficiently.

## Creating your store

You have two ways to get started:

| Method | Best for |
| --- | --- |
| **Self-service signup** | Pharmacy owners who want to explore the platform immediately |
| **Assisted onboarding** | Teams who want help with data migration, configuration, and staff training |

### Self-service signup

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to the [Start Free Trial](https://www.mediflux.in/start-free-trial) page and enter your pharmacy details, GSTIN, and business type.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Your cloud store is provisioned instantly — **no downloads, no servers, no IT team required.**
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Log in and start exploring the dashboard right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Assisted onboarding

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Fill out the form on the [Start Free Trial](https://www.mediflux.in/start-free-trial) page with your pharmacy details.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Our support team will contact you and guide you through the setup process.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    We connect remotely to help you understand the system and give you a live walkthrough.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    We assist with importing or manually adding your stock and customer records.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    We guide you through billing, expiry alerts, and staff role configuration.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Training is provided remotely so your team can start using **MediFlux** confidently from day one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configuring your store

Once your store is created, configure the basics before you start billing:

| Setting | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| **GST rates** | Set your default GST rates and HSN preferences under **Settings → Accounting Settings** |
| **Invoice templates** | Choose your bill format under **Settings → Bill Templates** |
| **Payment methods** | Configure accepted payment methods and linked bank accounts under **Settings → Accounting Settings** |
| **Customer credit policies** | Set credit limits and payment terms if you offer Udhaar (credit) sales |
| **User roles** | Add staff members and assign permissions under **Settings → User Management** |

## Adding your inventory

You can populate your drug catalog in three ways:

| Method | How it works |
| --- | --- |
| **Excel import** | Upload your existing drug list via Excel. **MediFlux** pre-fills drug names, compositions, and HSN codes where available. |
| **Barcode scan** | Scan drug barcodes to auto-populate name, batch, expiry, MRP, and stock details. |
| **Manual entry** | Add medicines one by one through the **Drugs → Add Drug** form. |

<Figure

## Making your first sale

Once your inventory is set up, you're ready to bill:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **Sales → Add Sale** and select **Retail** or **Wholesale** mode.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Add a customer (or leave as cash sale) and select drugs from your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Choose the payment status — **Paid**, **Unpaid**, or **Partial**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **+ Add** to finalise the bill.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Figure
  src="/images/getting-started-add-sale.png"
  title="Add Sale"
  description="The Retail sale form with customer details, payment status, and drug table ready for billing."
/>

Your first sale is now recorded. You can view it anytime in [Sales List](/docs/sales/sales-list), share it via WhatsApp, or print it directly.

## Moving to a paid subscription

After your free trial, our team will guide you through purchasing a subscription. We offer **transparent yearly and multi-year plans with no hidden fees or surprise charges.** Payment can be made via UPI, net banking, or other secure channels.

| Step | What happens |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Receive your invoice and make payment through your preferred mode |
| 2 | **Subscription activates instantly** upon payment confirmation |
| 3 | Full access and continuous support continues with no downtime |

With your paid subscription, you get access to all advanced features, regular updates, and our support team whenever you need help.

## What's next

Once your setup is complete, explore these features to get the most out of **MediFlux**:

<Figure
  src="/images/getting-started-dashboard.png"
  title="Dashboard Overview"
  description="Your home screen shows key metrics — total drugs, drug value, suppliers, customers, and expired stock alerts."
/>

- **Expiry and stock alerts** — Get notified before drugs expire or run low
- **Sales analytics** — Track trends, profit margins, and top-selling drugs from the dashboard
- **GST reports** — Generate GSTR-1, GSTR-2, and GSTR-3B with one click
- **Customer and supplier ledgers** — Track credit, payments, and outstanding dues
- **Role-based access** — Control what each staff member can view and edit

<Callout type="info" title="Quick tip">
Click the **+ button** in the top bar from any page to quickly add a Sale, Purchase, Customer, Customer Payment, Supplier, or Supplier Payment without navigating through menus.
</Callout>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="How long does the free trial last?">
    The free trial gives you **full access for 7 days** with no credit card required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch from assisted onboarding to self-service later?">
    Yes. Even if you start with assisted onboarding, you retain full self-service access to all settings and features at any time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I don't subscribe after the trial?">
    Your data remains in the system for a limited grace period. We recommend subscribing before the trial ends to avoid any interruption. Contact support if you need an extension.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a GSTIN to use MediFlux?">
    **No.** While entering your GSTIN enables GST-compliant invoicing and reporting, you can use **MediFlux** for basic billing and inventory without it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use MediFlux on multiple devices?">
    **Yes.** **MediFlux** syncs across all devices in real time. You can bill from your shop computer, check stock from your phone, and manage reports from a tablet.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
