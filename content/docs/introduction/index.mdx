# Introduction to MediFlux

MediFlux is the **pharmacy management and billing software** trusted by **5,000+ pharmacies** across India. Built specifically for the Indian healthcare market, it unifies everything a pharmacy needs — from lightning-fast GST billing and barcode scanning to real-time inventory tracking, expiry management, customer and supplier ledgers, advanced accounting and intelligent analytics into one intuitive, cloud-native platform.

Whether you run a single medical retail counter, a wholesale distribution business, or a clinic with an attached dispensary, MediFlux eliminates the operational chaos that slows you down and replaces it with clarity, speed, and control.

## How MediFlux Works

Getting started with MediFlux takes minutes, not days. The platform is designed to get you from signup to your first sale with minimal friction.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your store">
    [Sign up at MediFlux](https://app.mediflux.in/registration), enter your pharmacy details, GSTIN, and business type. Your cloud store is provisioned instantly — no downloads, no servers, no IT team required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import or add your inventory">
    Upload your existing drug list via Excel, scan barcodes to auto-populate details, or add medicines manually. MediFlux pre-fills drug names, compositions, and HSN codes where available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure GST & billing preferences">
    Set your default GST rates, invoice templates, payment methods, and customer credit policies. Everything is customizable to match how your store actually operates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start billing & managing">
    Begin creating sales, managing purchases, tracking expiry, and monitoring your business health from the unified dashboard. Your data syncs across all devices in real time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Security & Compliance

Your pharmacy data is among your most valuable assets. MediFlux treats it that way.

- **Role-based access control** — define who can view, edit, or approve transactions
- **Automatic cloud backups** with 30-day retention
- **GST-compliant audit trails** — every transaction is logged and immutable
- **SOC 2-aligned security practices** and regular third-party penetration testing

For a detailed breakdown of our security architecture, data handling policies, and compliance certifications, see our [Security & Privacy](/docs/introduction/security) documentation.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Is MediFlux suitable for a single medical shop?">
    Absolutely. MediFlux is designed to be powerful enough for large distributors yet simple enough for a single-counter medical store. You only pay for what you use, and the interface scales to your needs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need technical knowledge to use MediFlux?">
    No. MediFlux is built for pharmacy owners and staff, not IT professionals. The interface is intuitive, and most users are billing confidently within their first hour. We also provide free onboarding support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use MediFlux on my mobile phone?">
    Yes. MediFlux is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Access your store from any device with a browser.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is my data safe if I stop using MediFlux?">
    Yes. You can export all your data — inventory, sales, purchases, ledgers — at any time in standard formats (Excel, CSV, PDF). We never lock you in.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does MediFlux work on slow internet?">
    Yes. MediFlux is optimized for Indian network conditions. We intelligently cache data, compress payloads, and batch sync requests so the app stays fast and responsive even on 2G or unstable broadband connections. A stable internet connection is required, but slow speeds won't hold you back.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens to my data during GST audits?">
    MediFlux maintains complete, immutable audit trails for every transaction. Generate GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and detailed sales/purchase registers instantly — fully compliant with Indian GST regulations.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
