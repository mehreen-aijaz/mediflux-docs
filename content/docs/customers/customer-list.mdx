The **Customer List** is your main directory of every customer linked to your pharmacy — both individual consumers and businesses. It gives you a real-time snapshot of outstanding dues, customer ratings, and contact details, with the flexibility to view your customers as a list or as cards.

---

## Summary cards

At the top of the page, four summary cards give you an at-a-glance overview of your entire customer base:

| Card | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Total Customers** | Total number of customer profiles in your system |
| **Outstanding** | Total amount owed to you across all customers |
| **Linked Stores** | Number of customers who are other pharmacy stores linked through MediFlux |
| **Local Customers** | Number of manually added local/individual customers |

## Searching customers

The search bar at the top lets you search across **names, phone numbers, emails, and addresses**. Just start typing and the list filters instantly.

To the left of the search bar is an **All** dropdown — use it to narrow the search scope if needed.

  src="/images/customer-search.png"

## Filter chips

Below the search bar, two quick-filter chips let you slice the list instantly:

| Filter | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Linked Stores** | Only customers who are other pharmacy stores linked via MediFlux |
| **Local Customers** | Only manually added individual or business customers |

## Switching between List and Grid view

Next to the filter chips, two view icons let you switch how customers are displayed:

- **Grid view** — shows each customer as a card with their rating badge, name, type (Consumer/Business), phone, email, address, and how long they've been a customer
- **List view** — shows customers in a compact table format

## Customer Rating badges

Every customer card or row shows a rating badge based on the **Customer Rating** stars assigned when the customer was added or edited:

| Badge | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **None** | No rating assigned |
| **Gold** | A high rating assigned to the customer |

## The customer table (List view)

| Column | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Name** | Customer name, with a **Linked Store** tag if applicable |
| **Age** | Customer's age, if added. Shows `—` if not provided |
| **Gender** | Customer's gender, if added. Shows `—` if not provided |
| **Phone** | Primary contact number |
| **Email** | Customer's email address |
| **Outstanding** | Amount currently owed by this customer, shown in red |
| **Last Payment** | Date of the customer's last payment. Shows `—` if none recorded |
| **Actions** | Quick action menu for that customer row |

## Sorting the table

The **Name** and **Outstanding** columns are sortable. Click the column header to toggle between ascending and descending order.

| Option | What it does |

| **Sort By** | Sort customers by Name or Last Payment |
| **Last Payment Date Range** | Filter customers by a Start Date and End Date based on their last payment |

Click **Apply Filters** to apply, or **Reset** to clear all filters.

## Exporting customers

Click the **export icon** to open the **Export Customers** dialog.

### Columns to include

Choose which columns to include in the export. **Name** is always included by default. You can select or deselect:

`GSTIN`, `License Number`, `Phone`, `Address`, `Created At`, `Last Payment Date`, `Last Bill Date`, `Current Outstanding`

Click **Deselect All** to clear all selections at once.

### Filter by date ranges

Filter the export by three independent date ranges, each with quick options — **All Time, This Month, Last Month, This Quarter, This Year, Last Year** — or a custom **From** and **To** date:

- **Created At**
- **Last Payment Date**
- **Last Bill Date**

### Filter by outstanding

| Option | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Has outstanding** | Only include customers with a pending balance |
| **No outstanding** | Only include customers with a zero balance |
| **All** | Include all customers regardless of balance |

You can also set a **Min (₹)** and **Max (₹)** outstanding amount range.

### Sort by

Choose a field and order (ascending or descending) to sort the exported data.

### Export format

Click the dropdown arrow on the **Export** button to choose:

- **Export PDF**
- **Export Excel**
