When another pharmacy store on MediFlux adds you as their **supplier** (via the Link Supplier option in their Suppliers section), a request is sent to you. This request appears on the **Store Requests** page, where you can choose to accept or reject the connection.

Navigate here from the sidebar: **Customers → Store Requests**.

---

## How store requests work

When another pharmacy store on MediFlux uses **Link Supplier** to connect with your store, a request is sent to you. It appears on your **Store Requests** page, showing the requesting store's name, contact person, phone number, email, and address. From here you can **Accept** or **Reject** the connection — accepting adds them to your Customer List as a **Linked Store**.

---

## Viewing a request

Each request is shown as a card with the following details:

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Store Name** | Name of the pharmacy requesting to link with you |
| **Contact Person** | Name of the contact person at that store |
| **Phone Number** | Their contact number |
| **Email** | Their email address |
| **Address** | Their store address |
| **Request Date** | When the request was sent |

## Accepting a request

Click **Accept** on a request card. A confirmation dialog appears:

> **Accept Request?**
> Store manager will be able to see your store on his dashboard once you accept the request.

Click **Confirm** to accept, or **Cancel** to go back without accepting.

<Figure
  src="https://waibusiness.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/media/1783664090311-101534203-aceppt-request.png"
  title="Accept Request confirmation"
  description="Confirmation dialog before accepting a store link request."
/>

Once accepted, the store is added to your **Customer List** as a **Linked Store**, and they will be able to see your store as a supplier on their end.

---

## Rejecting a request

Click **Reject** on a request card. A confirmation dialog appears:

> **Reject Request?**
> You'll have to wait for the supplier to resubmit the request if you reject it.

Click **Confirm** to reject, or **Cancel** to go back without rejecting.

<Figure
  src="https://waibusiness.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/media/1783664090310-525795748-reject-request.png"
  title="Reject Request confirmation"
  description="Confirmation dialog before rejecting a store link request."
/>

<Note>
If you reject a request, the requesting store will need to send the link request again from their end if they want to reconnect.
</Note>

---
