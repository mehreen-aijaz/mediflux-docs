The **Prescriptions** page keeps a complete record of every prescription issued by your clinic's doctors. Each prescription is linked to the doctor who wrote it, making it easy to track clinical activity and review patient care history.

---
## will be updated soon
## What you can do

- **View all prescriptions** — See every prescription issued, with patient details, doctor name, date, and medicines prescribed
- **Search prescriptions** — Find prescriptions by patient name, doctor name, or date
- **Filter by doctor** — Narrow down prescriptions to see only those written by a specific doctor
- **View prescription details** — Click any prescription to see the full list of medicines, dosage instructions, and notes

---

## The prescriptions table

| Column | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Date** | When the prescription was issued |
| **Patient** | Name of the patient |
| **Doctor** | Doctor who wrote the prescription |
| **Medicines** | Number of medicines prescribed |
| **Actions** | View full prescription details |

---

## Viewing a prescription

Click on any prescription row to open the full details, showing:

| Section | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Patient Information** | Name, age, phone, and address |
| **Doctor Details** | Name, registration number, and qualification |
| **Prescribed Medicines** | Medicine name, dosage, frequency, and duration |
| **Instructions** | Special notes or advice from the doctor |
| **Date & Time** | When the prescription was created |
